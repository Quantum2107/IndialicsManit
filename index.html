About Conference

Advancing Innovation & Development In The Global South

The IndiaLICS Annual International Conference 2026 brings together scholars, policymakers, industry leaders, development practitioners, and innovation researchers to explore how innovation systems, infrastructure, and capital markets can contribute to sustainable and inclusive development in the Global South.

Hosted by MANIT Bhopal in collaboration with IndiaLICS and the broader GLOBELICS network, the conference aims to create a vibrant platform for interdisciplinary dialogue, research exchange, policy engagement, and collaborative learning.

About IndiaLICS

India Learning Innovation and Competence Building Systems

IndiaLICS is a national network of scholars, researchers, policymakers, and practitioners committed to advancing research on innovation, learning, competence building, and development.

The network serves as a platform for knowledge exchange and collaborative research while fostering engagement among academia, industry, government, and civil society organizations.

IndiaLICS is affiliated with the international GLOBELICS network and contributes significantly to understanding innovation and development challenges within the Global South.

About GLOBELICS

GLOBELICS is an international network dedicated to strengthening research and capacity building in the field of innovation systems and economic development.

The network connects scholars from developed and developing economies and promotes understanding of innovation as a critical driver of inclusive growth, sustainability, and societal transformation.

Through conferences, workshops, doctoral academies, and collaborative projects, GLOBELICS supports evidence-based policymaking and international research partnerships.

Host Institution

Maulana Azad National Institute of Technology

Maulana Azad National Institute of Technology (MANIT), Bhopal, is one of India's premier technical institutions and an Institute of National Importance.

Established in 1960 and granted NIT status in 2002, MANIT has consistently contributed to excellence in education, research, innovation, and societal development.

Spread across a vast green campus in the heart of Bhopal, MANIT offers a vibrant academic ecosystem supporting interdisciplinary learning and cutting-edge research.
